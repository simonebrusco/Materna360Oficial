## ‚ú® General Tone Guidelines
1. **Emotional direction:**  
   Gentle, confident, and reassuring ‚Äî speaks to mothers who are doing their best amid daily challenges.

2. **Style rules:**  
   - Short, human sentences (max two lines per message).  
   - Avoid exclamation marks, ellipses, or diminutives.  
   - Replace emojis with Lucide icons when emphasis is needed.  
   - Avoid slang or technical jargon.  
   - Use inclusive and positive language ("voc√™ est√° fazendo o melhor poss√≠vel").  

3. **Hierarchy:**  
   - Titles: sentence case, semibold, calm.  
   - Subtitles: regular weight, slightly smaller, two-line max.  
   - CTAs: concise and active (e.g., "Acessar ‚Üí", "Adicionar item", "Salvar").  

4. **Empty States:**  
   - Should always encourage, never scold.  
   - Offer one simple next step ("Comece com algo leve").  
   - Prefer warm imperatives ("Conte um pouco", "Planeje pequenas tarefas").
